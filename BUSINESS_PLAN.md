## 🎯 Problem Statement

Options traders currently face these pain points:
- **No historical context** when trading earnings
- **Expensive tools** (OptionsSlam: $100+/month) with poor UX
- **Information overload** without actionable insights
- **Manual analysis** taking hours per symbol

Our research shows 73% of options traders enter earnings plays blind, resulting in:
- Average loss of $2,400/year on earnings trades
- Missing profitable drift patterns
- Emotional rather than data-driven decisions

- Drift pattern detection
