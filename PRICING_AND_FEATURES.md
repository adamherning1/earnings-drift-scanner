## What's Included in Every Playbook:

1. **Quick Historical Stats** (5 bullet points)
   - Win rate with dollar amounts
   - Average drift % and $ move
   - Best/worst historical moves
   - Current IV ranking

2. **Three Specific Trades**
   - Aggressive play (higher risk/reward)
   - Conservative play (spreads)
   - Hedge recommendation

3. **Exact Instructions**
   - Contract quantity based on account size
   - Entry time (e.g., Monday 9:45 AM)
   - Exit time (e.g., Wednesday 3:30 PM)
   - Dollar risk and target amounts
